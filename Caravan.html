Caravan

Caravan is a network for knowledge workers seeking new jobs that value entrepreneurial skills.

Caravan is in private alpha.
Here's how it works:

  • 1. You sign up and tell us if you are hiring or want to be hired.
  • 2. We interview you to capture information for your profile. We build a profile for you, and after your approval your profile is live on Caravan.
  • 3. We work to connect members who want new jobs with members who want to hire entrepreneurs.
